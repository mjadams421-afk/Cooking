Nutrition Science

Food plays a big part in overall health of a human body. In general, fat and sugar cause major problems to a body over time. There are many foods that have only beneficial value to a body.

The ones to avoid are:

  1. High fat content Causes cholesterol buildup which leads to heart disease and issues with digestion
  2. High sugar content Causes diabetetic sysmptoms and issues with digestion

More than 70 foods are healing foods. These foods include major nutrients such as Vitamin A,B,C,D,E; Potassium; Iron; Zinc; Calcium; Magnesium and Selenium.

  1. Vitamin A [carotene which is non toxic at large dose] Found in carrots, sweet potatoes, lettuce & tomato. Helps with immune system and carotene helps prevent certain cancers
  2. Vitamin B1,2,3 [Majors known as thiamine, riboflavin & niacin] Found in whole grains, meat, fish & vegtables. Helps to metabolize protiein, carbohydrates & fats
  3. Vitamin B6,12 [Minors known as pyridoxine & cyanocobalamin] Found in meat, fish & dairy. Helps with blood production, normal growth, brain function & nervous system
  4. Vitamin C Found in citrus fruits, beans, spinach and lettuce. Helps with immune system, iron absorption, mental health and preventing some types of cancer
  5. Vitamin D [vitamin and hormone] Primary source is sunlight. Helps with calcium absorption and preventing some types of cancer
  6. Vitamin E Primary source is supplements. Helps with metabolism of polystaurated fats and preventing some types of cancer
  7. Potassium Found in bananas, meats and tomatoes. Helps with synthesizing proteins and blood pressure regulation by counteracting the effects of sodium
  8. Iron [overdose may cause iron poisoning]Found in red meat, bran, beans and seeds. Helps with building red blood cells and some enzymes
  9. Zinc Found in meat, fish, dairy and wheat. Helps with building some enzymes, building bones, normal growth and increase resistance to infection
  10. Calcium [Overdose may cause kidney stones] Found in dairy, greens and beans. Helps with bone health, cardiac health and preventing some types of cancer
  11. Magnesium Found in banana, carrots, fish and meat. Helps with nervous system, cardivascular health and preventing osteoporosis
  12. Selenium [Overdose may cause selenium poisoning]Found in meat, fish, garlic, asparagus and mushrooms. Helps maintain red blood cells and preventing congestive heart failure

Cook Fish

Baked Fish Filet of Salmon or river fish

  1. Place filet on baking pan with olive oilskin down on pan
  2. mix in a bowl olive oil,lemon juice, garlic, herbs, salt & pepper
  3. Pour mixture on top of filet
  4. sprinkle bread crumbs light on top of fish
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for 25-27 minutesdepending on size of fish

Tuna Cassarole

  1. Put olive oil in a large baking pan
  2. Mix in pan 1 can of cream of mushroom soup, 1 can of cream of chicken soup, 2 cans of mushrooms & 4 cans of tuna
  3. On the side boil 2 boxes of pasta with salt and pepper
  4. Mix in baking pan 1/2 chopped onion, 1 cup of cheese, salt, pepper & splash of milk
  5. Drain pasta and mix in baking pan
  6. Sprinkle a covering of cheese on top
  7. Bake at 400 degrees for 40 minutes
